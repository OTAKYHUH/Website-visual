MODULE 5 – Activity Management

Different yard activities require different actions. As a YAS, it is your responsibility to understand and respond with the best supervisory action.

Upon checking on W25 activity, mounting activity is for non-urgent IYS. What should you do?

1) Call IYS to suspend and inform the Yard Planner to replan offloading

2) Call IYS to suspend and inform YOC to assign an RTG for offloading

3) Call IYS to suspend and inform YAS to replan (kill the block)

4) None of the above

Ideal situation during Peak and Lull period:

Peak / Heavy Lull / Light
  • Inform all shifting team (IYS, ITT, IGH) to suspend non-urgent shifting
  • Workthru all YCO
  • Request for additional holding area from YPDM
  • Planner to raise ITT boxes to ease yard space
  • Deploy optimal number of YCOs
  • Kill end block to allow crane parking (e.g. PPT2 S1: T & Y; PPT3: S & Y)
  • Plan for YCOs’ meal breaks during lull periods
  • Consider shifting support and identify required blocks
  • Able to proceed with workthru; coordination with the shifting team is needed to identify executable blocks
