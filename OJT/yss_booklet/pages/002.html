INTRODUCTION

This OJT program is designed to equip you with the operational expertise and safety competencies required as a Yard Shifting Supervisor in YOD. By completing this training, you will master operational and safety competencies critical to our yard operations.

Use this booklet as your daily reference guide during the 4-week program.

TRAINING OBJECTIVES

Upon successful completion of this program, you will be competent to:

Execute Deployment Verification
  • Conduct pre-shift checks of equipment and personnel deployment
  • Check for current CMRs available for the shift and Point Group Allocations
Develop Shifting Plans
  • Create optimized work plans based on:
    • Available CMR/PM resources
    • Operational urgency priorities
    • Vessel cut-off timelines
Maximize Yard Productivity
  • Strategically allocate PM resources to:
    • Achieve IYS performance targets such as IYS wait time and Productivity levels which contributes to overall Yard KPIs
    • Minimize empty trips where possible to increase operational efficiency
    • Efficiently resolve overstows and long hauls
Maintain Safety Compliance
  • Implement all PSA safety protocols during:
    • Equipment operations
    • Fatigue management of PM drivers
    • Personnel movements
