5.3 : Gantry RTG
5.3.1 : Linear Gantry
*  Immediately call RTG operator to “STOP” gantry, if observe any safety infringement and escalate to ɣQDM
Gantry Path CCTV Camera Views
YOMC RTG Gantry Instruction
  • Tune to the gantry path CCTV camera views
  • Send RTG gantry instruction via YOMC
  • RTG operator check for obstruction on the gantry path, gantry to the last 40-footer slot (or 1st 40 footer slot) of the block and stop.
RTG Block Channel
RTG at RED/WHITE LINE
  • Switch UHF set to RTG block channel (wait for RTG operator to call RP).
  • RP Supervisor check for clearance, inform RTG operator to “Inch” out to the RED/WHITE line and STOP.
  • RP Supervisor check for clearance on the street to the intended block (ensure that vehicles awaiting RTG gantry had hazard lights on before instructing RTG to gantry out of the block).
  • Once cleared, inform RTG operator to activate the Siren and gantry on the street to the intended block.
  • Once inside the intended block, key “arrived”, switch UHF to the block channel and continue with operations in the block.
