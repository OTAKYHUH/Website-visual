2.2.3 : Automated Rail Mounted Gantry (ARMG)

Located at ARMG yard, handling both laden and unladen containers. Fully automated during mounting and offloading of containers.

2.2.4 : Hyster / FM / Reach Stacker

Located at hyster yard, FM handling empty containers only and reach stacker handling both laden and empty containers. Each equipment is handled by operator manually during mounting and offloading of containers. Door turning operation will be done by reach stacker using CST prime mover only.
