Understanding of the Indication:
Types of Containers Description Opening the block without selecting any vessel cluster Opening the block by selecting the vessel cluster
Landed Container Container that lied / landed in the location. Can be identified by the strip lines.
Planned Container Container that planned to move to the location. No stripe lines will be shown in the planned container.
Shut Container Container that will not be loaded onto the vessel. May be due to change in the loading vessel.
ATM Container Container that has a long stay in the yard. There is neither a specific port mark nor the port stays of the container.
