Task 8: Diversion Basic Knowledge
8.1: PSCW
Port Mark
Port Mark refers to designated discharging port of the vessel. Containers should not be stack if they are from different port mark within the same vessel.
Size
Size refers to the length of the container.
Do not mix 20ft, 40ft and 45ft.
Category
Category refers to the type of container.
Do not mix different types of container type.
GP, HC, FE, FR, TK, OH/OW/HW and DG.
Weight
The lighter container should not be stacked on top of the heavier container. The weight indication for sea is in tonnage. Light (3-9.9), Medium (10-14.9), Heavy (15-19.9), Xtra (20-24.9) and Ultra Heavy (≥25) Below table shows the weight class of the container weighing from the heaviest to the lightest.
