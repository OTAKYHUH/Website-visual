Task 9: Yard Exceptions
9.1: Handle and Escalate Different Yard Exceptions
This task helps you to identify, handle, and escalate various yard exceptions to maintain operational continuity and safety. You will learn protocols for common exceptions like misplaced containers, equipment failures, and hazardous situations.
Learning Objectives:
  • Recognize different types of yard exceptions
  • Execute proper handling procedures
  • Apply escalation protocols
  • Document exceptions accurately
  • Coordinate with relevant teams
