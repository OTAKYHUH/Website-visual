INTRODUCTION

A YAS (yard activity supervisor) is responsible for managing and supervising daily operations related to yard matters. YAS serves as the crucial bridge between yard planning and execution. Replanning of container is necessary during equipment breakdowns, traffic congestion or unexpected container issues. The goal is to ensure efficiency and proper coordination of yard activities.

TRAINING OBJECTIVES

Upon successful completion of this program, you will be competent to:

  • Understand the role of a Yard Activity Supervisor
  • Able to define the responsibilities of a YAS as the link between yard planning and ground execution
  • Recognise the importance of situational awareness and real-time decision making in yard operations
  • Apply Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)
  • Demonstrate working knowledge of PSA’s yard SOPs as follows
  • Stowage category e.g DG (dangerous goods), FR (flat rack) and TK (tank)
  • Container loading port code
  • Container weight class e.g light, medium or heavy
  • Intended movement type like ITH or FCL
  • Coordinate Yard Operations Effectively
  • Communicate clearly with crane operators, prime mover drivers, planning team and control center personnel
  • Monitor activities to ensure smooth and efficient container flow
  • Respond to Operational Challenges
  • Support timely replanning and escalate when necessary
  • Identify common disruption e.g equipment issues
