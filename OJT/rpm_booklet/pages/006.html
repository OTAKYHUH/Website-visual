Competency Assessment Checklist for RP

At the end of OJT, a mentor will be assigned to conduct the competency assessment to determine the current standing of the trainee. The mentor is responsible for assessing the trainee on the individual tasks and filling in the performance standards between a scale of 0 – 4.

1 – Need more practice, 2 – Pass, 3 – Good, 4 – Excellent

Task Outcome Performance Standard Mentor’s Comments
1 2 3 4
PERFORMANCE ASSESSMENT
1. Perform Basic Terminal Operations and Monitor Yard Key Performance Indicators (KPI)
Know Control Centre (CC) staff guidelines, different staff roles and contact list
Know Basic Terminal Operations flow
Know Yard KPIs and the tools to monitor them via yard wait time excel and utilize PM NO SHOW panel
2. Identify Yard Layout and Equipment
Able to explain Yard Layout and Yard details
Able to explain Yard Equipment and basic mode of operations
3. Use of UHF set and CCTV for Operations
Tune to RTG block channels
Tune to RP and SOC channels
Private call numbers for PM / RTG / FM / Reach Stacker
Familiarize with Yard CCTV numbers
4. Use of Systems for Operations
Familiar with CITOS functions
Familiar with use of CITIS functions
YOMC – Understand all possible functions of and use to monitor yard operations
Familiar with use of logical yard and yard QCC
Familiar with use of CANS
VOMC - Understand vessel sequence and use VPA to view incoming yard activities
5. Gantry of RTGs and Yard Safety SOPs
Tune to correct RTG block channels
Ensure RTG gantry safely and correctly
Understand Yard Safety SOPs
Escalation of any safely infringement to necessary parities
6. Monitor Yard Operations and Equipment
Use YOMC to monitor ship ops mounting / offloading
Differentiate and understand how to handle L&R, Overland, Shut Out
Understand every non-ship ops yard operation and how to manage their activities
Explain workings of ERTG / OH / RTG / FM / ARMG and their differences
Understand deployment sheet
7. Forecast RTG Yard Activities and Equipment Positioning
Reposition RTGs matching operator skill to yard activities
Use of RTG activities forecast email to position RTG for heavy yard activities
Able to differentiate and use both YOMC job list and activity list
Use of logical yard (enhance activities) to forecast incoming activities
Understand PM diversion SOP and Divert PMs correctly
8. Prime Mover (PM) Diversions
Identify different types of container categories
PM Diversion SOPs
9. Handle and Escalate Different Yard Exceptions
Missing container / Wrongly updated container
Leaking DG or Non-DG container
Reported damaged container
Spillage in Yard and Bund SOP
PM Breakdown
PM driver reported mounting / offloading different container number
PM driver reported container wrongly declared (size or category)
PM driver uncontactable
IYS PM driver does not deploy the following shift
System / PC related issues
ARMG or RTG breakdown / PTW (crane servicing) / TID (lane maintenance work)
Manual yard moves
SUPERVISORY SKILLS ASSESSMENT
10. Manage Resources
Knows the strategies and guidelines to achieve KPI targets
11. Solve Problems and Make Decisions
Identify relevant issues and causes of problems
Make effective and practical decisions
Show firmness and impartiality during course of work
Explain the rationale behind work processes
ATTITUDE ASSESSMENT
12. Provide Customer Service
Answer customer queries and be able to direct the proper personnel involved
Phone etiquette handling customers
13. Manage Job Performance
Conscientious in execution of duties and responsibilities
Willing and able to change his way of working when required
Willing to perform more than what is required of his assignment and job without being told to do so
